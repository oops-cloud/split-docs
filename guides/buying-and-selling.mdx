## Buy

Enter SOL amount, click Buy, one wallet popup. You're auto-registered for rewards in the same transaction.

## Sell

Enter token amount or use percentage buttons. The transaction snapshots your rewards before executing the swap.

<Warning>
  100% sell resets your multiplier. Partial sells do not.
</Warning>

## Fees

1.2% to all holders, 0.4% to protocol, 0.4% to Meteora. Same on every DEX.

## External trading

Works on Jupiter, Axiom, Photon, BullX — any Solana DEX. External traders auto-registered by crank within ~60s.
