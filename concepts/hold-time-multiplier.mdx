---
title: "Hold-Time Multiplier"
description: "Hold longer, earn more."
---

Your fee share scales with hold duration. 1x at day 0, 5x at day 30.

| Days | Multiplier |
| ---- | ---------- |
| 0    | 1.0x       |
| 7    | 1.93x      |
| 15   | 3.0x       |
| 30+  | 5.0x       |

## Example

Alice and Bob both hold 10M tokens. Alice has held for 30 days (5x), Bob for 1 day (1.13x).

1 SOL deposited: Alice gets 0.816 SOL, Bob gets 0.184 SOL.

## Resets

- Claiming rewards: **no reset**
- Buying more: **no reset**
- Selling 100%: **resets to 1x**
- Partial sell: **selling 50% or more in one transaction triggers a reset**
